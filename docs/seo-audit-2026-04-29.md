## CRITICAL FINDING BEFORE ALL OTHERS: Two Live Production Sites

**Priority: CRITICAL | Fix before any other optimization**

Both `https://www.a1qualityparalegal.com` (Wix, legacy) and `https://www.a1paralegal.com` (Astro/GitHub Pages, new) are live, indexed, and returning HTTP 200. This single issue undermines every other optimization effort:

- **Link equity is split** — backlinks to either domain do not consolidate
- **GBP can only point to one URL** — if it still points to the Wix site, all GBP traffic lands on the wrong page
- **NAP consistency is unmanageable** — citations reference two different domains and address formats
- **Duplicate content risk** — Google may treat both sites as competing signals for the same business
- **Neither site has a cross-domain canonical or redirect** — Google sees two independent properties

**Fix:** Set up a 301 permanent redirect from all `a1qualityparalegal.com` URLs to their equivalents on `a1paralegal.com`. This must be done at the domain/DNS level (Wix redirect rules or domain forwarding). Then update the GBP website URL to `https://www.a1paralegal.com` immediately.

**Until this is resolved, every optimization below has diminished return.**
